## 7. Replication Playbook

Based on what the top 3 closers (Eleni, Craig, Perry) do:

### The Winning Formula

1. **Speed**: Text within the same day of contact — 97%+ same-day rate. Top closers never wait.

2. **First text**: Send a Value Prop message (not a nudge). **Never include links.** Time it during the golden window (morning 9-11 AM or evening 5-7 PM local time).

3. **Persistence**: If no reply, send a second text (double-tap) within the same day or next morning. After 2 texts with no reply, the hazard rate drops 59% — stop at 2.

4. **Response speed**: When a lead replies, **respond within 30 minutes**. Won deals averaged 27-minute response vs 994 minutes for losses. This is the single biggest controllable lever.

5. **Call integration**: Use a blended approach. Top performers maintain 28-30:1 SMS-to-call ratios (not 200:1 like Ryan). Jason Ainsworth shows that calls alone (8.4/day) don't convert without good SMS discipline.

6. **Coverage**: Text 95%+ of contacted leads within 24 hours. Russell Armitage's 99% coverage + 83% bookend adherence drives the highest volume at respectable conversion rates.
